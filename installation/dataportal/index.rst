Dataportal
==========

The Dataportal is the user interface of the DAF, and add additional functionalities to the big data infrastructure, such as: a dataset catalog, content management system, business intelligence tools, an interactive notebook to perform data analysis and much more. It is made of two components: a public webapp (Dataportal-public) to serve public content, and a private one (Dataportal-private) to access analytics tools and other content creation services.
